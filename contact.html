Get Involved

Emajjin Children's Foundation is a registered charity operating solely through a group of dedicated volunteers. We avidly promote partnerships with the community.

We value and honour the various skill sets that volunteers bring to the organization. Motivated volunteers who believe they have the power to make a difference in their community are welcome. Volunteering opportunities include marketing, administrative assistance, event planning, technical support, fundraising, and promoting corporate partnerships.

If you are interested in joining our team, please email us at info@emajjin.org.
