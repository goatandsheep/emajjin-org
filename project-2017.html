2017: For the Love of Children Society

The mission of FTLOCS is to provide help, hope and support to disadvantaged children locally, regionally, and internationally. FTLOCS currently funds 63 orphanages and schools in India, Nepal, the Phillipines, Peru, Colombia, Afghanistan, Haiti, Honduras, Egypt, Lebanon, and Zambia. They have provided emergency disaster relief in Sri Lanka, Kashmir, Haiti, Mexico, Peru, and Sudan when the countries were affected by earthquakes, tsunamis, and droughts.

Your generosity at this gala will help rebuild an orphanage in Nepal that was destroyed by the 2015 earthquake. Funds raised will also be used to support schools in Cambodia and Peru.

For more information, click here to visit the For the Love of Children Society website.
